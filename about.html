The Professor

Dr. Leo Chalupa

Dr. Leo Chalupa is a distinguished scholar and academic leader who serves as a Professor of Pharmacology and Physiology at the GW School of Medicine and Health Sciences and a professor at the University Honors Program for undergraduate students. He also formerly served as the Vice President for Research at the George Washington University, and as the Chairman of the Department of Neurobiology, Physiology and Behavior at the University of California, Davis. An alumnus of Queen’s College in New York, Dr. Chalupa has had a rich career in the field of neuroscience.
As a professor, he has had an immense impact on his students and has been able to give them the space to explore subjects they are passionate about and obtain a good education from him. We are honored to have had him as our professor and have enjoyed our semester with him immensely.

Welcome to the official webpage of The Neuroscience Notebook. This podcast was made as a special project by honors students at the George Washington University as part of the class “History of Neuroscience” taught by Professor Leo Chalupa.

All of us students come from a variety of disciplines, but have been united by our common interest in how our minds shape various aspects of our existence. In this podcast, we have attempted to explore topics ranging from the metaphysical nature of our minds, how our minds influence our eating habits, how we are influenced by video games, and more.

Though this is by no means a comprehensive overview of the intricacies of our minds and our brains, we have had so much fun being able to explore this subject and present it in a way we hope is accessible to anyone and everyone with an interest in it.

Thank you so much for checking out The Neuroscience Notebook! This is a podcast put together by students at the George Washington University for the class “History of Neuroscience” in the Honors Program. We hope you enjoy diving into these various topics and the neuroscience behind them.
