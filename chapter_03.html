Chapter 03: Weight Loss and Appetite in the Brain

Presented By: Tanya Ryzi

Podcast Description: This episode explores the critical role that our brains play in regulating our food intake, metabolism, and body weight. It also discusses the physiological and psychological effects the brain imposes during calorie deprivation, and how this can be a barrier towards developing treatments for individuals seeking to lose weight.
