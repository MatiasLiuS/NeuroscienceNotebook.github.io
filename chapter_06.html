Chapter 06: Proprioception: The Eyes of the Body

Presented By: Priscilla Pang

Podcast Description: The sensation of your body in space is known as proprioception. In this chapter, we will talk about what proprioception is, an interesting case study, the history behind proprioception, and the role it plays in areas like prosthetics, zero gravity, and sports. Proprioception is a sense that is often overlooked, but it's very integral in the way we experience life.
