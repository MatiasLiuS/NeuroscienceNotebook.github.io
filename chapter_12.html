Chapter 12: The Hidden Malignancy of the Brain

By Katherine Phillips / 20 April 202 / 26:65

Chapter 12: The Hidden Malignancy of the Brain

Presented By: Katherine Phillips

Podcast Description: On this episode of the Neuroscience Notebook, The Hidden Malignancy of the Brain, we will unearth the history of the treatments for metastatic brain tumors. We will learn how they are different than other types of brain tumors and how their treatments also differ. We will learn about how immunotherapies generally work as well as how they originated. We will end with a discussion about the future ramifications for a cure and if it is possible. Join me on this episode to learn about and explore what treatment looks like for those diagnosed with brain tumors that have metastasized.
