Chapter 15: The Mystery of Plasticity: Can You Rewire the Brain?

Presented By: Radha Varadan

Podcast Description: What secrets does the brain reveal as it changes and evolves after serious damage? And how can we harness this power to treat brain injuries? All of these questions-- and more-- are the subject of this episode of the Neuroscience Notebook, as we take a deep dive into the concept of neuroplasticity. We'll tackle this investigation into neuroplasticity by examining some fascinating case studies and develop an understanding of how the brain can change and evolve in response to severe injuries and neurodegeneration. We'll take a look at the historical perspectives that have informed our current understanding of plasticity and explore the future implications of understanding this aspect of the brain. Is there a world where we cure Alzheimer's? Or perhaps have complete recoveries from strokes? All that, and more, on this episode of the Neuroscience Notebook.
